Our Story

Born from a love of
beautiful pastry.

INsweet started with a simple belief: that everyone in Yerevan deserves the kind of dessert that makes you pause before taking a bite.

Based in Arinj, Kotayk Marz, we bake every order fresh — from crisp butter-sablé tart shells to silky layered mousses with mirror-glaze finishes. Every recipe is rooted in French patisserie tradition, refined with seasonal ingredients and a genuine passion for flavour.

We don't have a walk-in café. We work directly with our customers through Instagram, phone, and email — because ordering something this personal should feel personal too.

How It Works

Ordering is easy
